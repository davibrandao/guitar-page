Some curiosities about guitar!

Most Expensive Guitar: The "Reach Out to Asia" guitar is said to be the most expensive guitar ever sold at auction.

Longest Guitar Solo: In 2011, David DiDonato set the world record for the longest guitar solo, playing for an incredible 24 hours, 33 minutes and 23 seconds.

First Solid Body Electric Guitar: The legendary Fender Telecaster guitar, introduced in 1951, is often considered the first mass produced solid body electric guitar.

Million Dollar Guitar: The "Eden of Coronet" guitar was created by guitar designer Aaron Shum and encrusted with over 11,000 diamonds.
